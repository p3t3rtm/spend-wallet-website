Seamless Payments, Two Ways

Our flexible POS protocol allows you to accept crypto payments through simple, intuitive methods that work for any retail environment.

Tap to Pay (NFC)

For in-person transactions, customers simply tap their NFC-enabled smartphone on your device. The payment is processed in seconds using Solana Pay.

App Links (QR Codes)

Generate a dynamic QR code on your terminal or device. Customers scan it with their phone, which instantly opens the Spend app to confirm the payment.
